About Albania

Located in Southeastern Europe, Albania (known as Shqipëria to locals) is bordered by Montenegro, Kosovo, North Macedonia, and Greece. Its capital city is Tirana. Kept largely isolated during the Cold War, it has rapidly emerged as one of Europe's most captivating travel destinations.

Must-Visit Places

Tirana

The vibrant and colorful capital city, known for Skanderbeg Square and its lively cafe culture.

Albanian Riviera

Crystal clear Ionian waters and beautiful coastal villages like Ksamil, Himarë, and Dhërmi.

Theth & Valbona

Dramatic peaks, deep valleys, and pristine nature found in the Albanian Alps in the north.
